#Created Measures Using DAX

#Example DAX formulas used:
- **Attrition Rate**:
  ```DAX
  Attrition Rate =DIVIDE(
    CALCULATE(COUNTROWS(FILTER(employee_summary, employee_summary[attrition_status] = "Yes"))),
    COUNTROWS(employee_summary)
)

- **Total Employees**:
  ```DAX
  Total Employees = DISTINCTCOUNT(Employee[EmployeeID])
  ```
